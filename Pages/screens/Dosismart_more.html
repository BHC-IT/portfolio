Dosismart is build with the help of dosimex. They kindly share with us their physics and nuclear engineering background.

Dosimex, as a excel based software, already have a huge adoption in the french industrie. We build on this to add modern features: historics, import/export, user group and more.

We are currently in private beta and hope to release a public beta soon.

The project is build with a microservice architecture. Most services are build in typescript, with c++ modules binded with node-gyp. The physics calculus service is build in Haskell.

All front application (mobile app, landing page, dashboard) are build with react.
